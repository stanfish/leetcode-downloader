<p>Given two integer arrays <code>A</code> and <code>B</code>, return the maximum length of an subarray that appears in both arrays.</p>

<p><b>Example 1:</b><br />
<pre>
<b>Input:</b>
A: [1,2,3,2,1]
B: [3,2,1,4,7]
<b>Output:</b> 3
<b>Explanation:</b> 
The repeated subarray with maximum length is [3, 2, 1].
</pre>
</p>

<p><b>Note:</b><br>
<ol>
<li>1 <= len(A), len(B) <= 1000</li>
<li>0 <= A[i], B[i] < 100</li>
</ol>
</p>
